👨‍💻 About Me

Data Scientist with over 5 years of experience in data, delivering data solutions across different industries, such as payments, banking, retail, and microbiology.

My work deeply combines technical applications (Machine Learning models, Generative AI products, dashboards, and reports) with the business sensitivity required to optimize journeys and deliver the best experience for customers.

Over the years, I have built a very solid foundation across different business contexts, working with Marketing, Product, Digital Channels, Customer Service, Sales, CRM, and currently Advanced Analytics teams.

Among my main achievements, I highlight:

  • Optimization of the digital onboarding journey, where, through the LSS methodology and exploratory data analysis, we reduced by 20% the volume of customers (MM) stuck in the flow in only 3 months of implementation. I present the methodology using hypothetical data in the project Case Onboarding Study.
  • Development of a D0 bug control report for the PIX and Wallet products in the app, where we moved from the Top 4 squads with incidents in the customer service center to Top 13 in only 2 months of use.
  • Delivery of an AI-powered Data Prep generator for ML models, where, through a Streamlit app + Claude Sonnet LLM, users fill out a form and then receive a .py file with the complete Data Prep, reducing development time from 30 to 15 days.
  • Creation of a toolkit for Genie Code (Databricks) that converts ML model notebooks into the MLflow production pipeline format, creating files in a maximum of 10 minutes.
